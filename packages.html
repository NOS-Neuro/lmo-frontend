How your VizAI Scan maps to a package

Your Discovery, Accuracy, and Authority scores are averaged to form a single visibility score from 0–100. That score drives the recommended plan.

80–100 · Strong visibility

AI likely recognizes and describes your business well. The risk is drift over time as models update and competitors invest. Basic gives you proactive monitoring and light monthly adjustments.

40–79 · Partial understanding

AI gets some parts right but misses key details or differentiators. Standard focuses on tightening your story, improving structure, and strengthening authority signals where it matters most.

0–39 · Critical risk

AI may not identify your business correctly, or may confuse you with others entirely. Standard + Add-Ons treats this as a deeper rebuild of how AI sees your brand, supported by an intensive project phase.
